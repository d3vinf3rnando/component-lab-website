How It Works

Three steps to zero QA debt

No setup. No configuration. Just select a component and run.

Click any component instance on your Figma canvas. Component Lab instantly detects all its properties, variants, and nested instances.

02

Choose your tests

Toggle which tests to run — Content & State, Variant, Nested Instance, or Theme. Mix and match based on what you need to verify.

03

Review the output

Component Lab generates organised frames directly on your canvas. Every state documented, every combination covered. Ready to share with your team.
